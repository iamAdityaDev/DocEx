# Notes App Documentation

## Overview

This app allows you to create, read, update, and delete notes. It also includes authentication that doesn't require personal information. All you need is a unique docex id and password. The UI is user-friendly and fully responsive.

## Usage

### Creating a Note

1. Click on the "Add a Note" button.
2. Enter the title and content of your note.
3. Click on the "Add Note" button.

### Reading a Note

1. Click on the note you want to read.
2. The title and content of the note will be displayed.

### Updating a Note

1. Click on the note you want to update.
2. Update the title and/or content of your note.
3. Click on the "Update" button.

### Deleting a Note

1. Click on the "Delete" button.

## Authentication

To authenticate, you need a unique docex id and password.
